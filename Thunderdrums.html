Thunderdrums

Thunderdrums are Tidal Class dragons. They have wide bodies and long wings. They have huge mouths like their wide bodies. Instead of fire, they have a roar so loud if you get hit by it, you cannot hear people for a while unless they are screaming. Because of this, "Thunderdrums are hard of hearing" quoting famous dragon rider Hiccup. When a baby Thunderdrum is hatching, it is said that it creats a tromendusly loud sound.
